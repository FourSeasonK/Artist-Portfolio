Time of the Monster

End and Start

Visual Artist

Sun K.

As society develops, there are many structural frameworks created under the name of 'quality of life.'
The resulting structural framework begins to limit not only behavior but our ability to perceive the very structures that impose themselves on us.

I want to express it as 'art' where what's being communicated is something beyond time and space,
something that can not be expressed in language or with any vast number,
something that happens where the body and mind meet with the 'unfamiliar' ; the lost freedom of the beginning.
