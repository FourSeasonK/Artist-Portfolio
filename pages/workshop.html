Making Mask

Expressing myself

While in Seoul, I put on an art workshop for actors and actresses.
We made a stage props related to a director's script.
We talked about their ideas for performance and we shared our inner selves and tried to match our identities with our props.
Finally, we created wonderful art props. They put on an excellent performance.
